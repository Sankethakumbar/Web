About INVENTRA

About the Hackathon Inventra 2K25 is a 24-hour national-level hackathon that aims to spark innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among aspiring engineers and technologists. The event provides a dynamic platform where participants brainstorm unique ideas, transform them into functional prototypes, and present impactful solutions to real-world challenges. Structured in three phases—Idea Evaluation, Prototype Development, and Final Presentation—the hackathon encourages originality, teamwork, and technical excellence. Beyond coding, Inventra 2K25 is about collaboration, learning, and pushing boundaries, while enjoying an engaging environment filled with challenges, checkpoints, mentoring, and fun activities. It is an opportunity for young innovators to showcase their skills, compete with the best, and make their mark as future changemakers.

Hackathon Domains

Generative AI

Explore the frontier of artificial intelligence! Build models that generate text, images, music, or code using AI techniques. This domain is perfect for innovators looking to create creative solutions and intelligent applications.Ideal for: AI enthusiasts, creative coders, and data scientists.

Supply Chain

Explore the future of the internet with decentralized applications, smart contracts, and new forms of digital ownership.

Full Stack

Dive into the world of web and app development! Work on both frontend and backend to create complete, scalable, and user-friendly applications. This domain is perfect for those who want to turn ideas into real working products.Ideal for: Web developers, app creators, and problem-solvers.

Internet Of Things

Connect the digital and physical worlds! Design smart devices, sensors, and systems that communicate seamlessly to automate and innovate. Ideal for building solutions for smart homes, cities, and industries. Ideal for: Hardware and software developers, innovators in automation.

Cyber Security

Step into the shoes of a cyber defender! Identify vulnerabilities, prevent attacks, and build secure systems. This domain challenges participants to create solutions for real-world cybersecurity threats.Ideal for: Security enthusiasts, ethical hackers, and system defenders.
