About INVENTRA

About the Hackathon
Inventra 2K25 is a 24-hour national-level hackathon that aims to spark innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among aspiring engineers and technologists. The event provides a dynamic platform where participants brainstorm unique ideas, transform them into functional prototypes, and present impactful solutions to real-world challenges. Structured in three phases—Idea Evaluation, Prototype Development, and Final Presentation—the hackathon encourages originality, teamwork, and technical excellence. Beyond coding, Inventra 2K25 is about collaboration, learning, and pushing boundaries, while enjoying an engaging environment filled with challenges, checkpoints, mentoring, and fun activities. It is an opportunity for young innovators to showcase their skills, compete with the best, and make their mark as future changemakers.

Why Participate?

Sure, the prize pool and goodies are exciting—but a hackathon is about so much more. It’s a 24-hour journey of creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving. You’ll push your limits, explore bold ideas, and experience the thrill of building something from scratch. Along the way, you’ll connect with like-minded peers, enjoy free food and caffeine, collect exclusive event swag, and create memories that last a lifetime. Whether you win or not, you’ll gain new skills, boost your confidence, and take home an experience that truly sets you apart.

Hackathon Domains

Generative AI

Explore the frontier of artificial intelligence! Build models that generate text, images, music, or code using AI techniques. This domain is perfect for innovators looking to create creative solutions and intelligent applications.

Ideal for: AI enthusiasts, creative coders, and data scientists.

Supply Chain

Explore innovative solutions to optimize logistics, track products in real time, and improve efficiency across industries. Leverage technology for transparent, secure, and automated supply chain systems.

Ideal for: Supply chain enthusiasts, logistics innovators, and tech-savvy problem solvers.

Full Stack

Dive into the world of web and app development! Work on both frontend and backend to create complete, scalable, and user-friendly applications. This domain is perfect for those who want to turn ideas into real working products.

Ideal for: Web developers, app creators, and problem-solvers.

Internet Of Things

Connect the digital and physical worlds! Design smart devices, sensors, and systems that communicate seamlessly to automate and innovate. Ideal for building solutions for smart homes, cities, and industries.

Ideal for: Hardware and software developers, innovators in automation.

Cyber Security

Step into the shoes of a cyber defender! Identify vulnerabilities, prevent attacks, and build secure systems. This domain challenges participants to create solutions for real-world cybersecurity threats.

Ideal for: Security enthusiasts, ethical hackers, and system defenders.
